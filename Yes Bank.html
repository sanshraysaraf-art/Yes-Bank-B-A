Crisis Timeline - Key Events

September 2018
Founder Rana Kapoor's Exit Announced

RBI restricts Rana Kapoor's term as MD & CEO, limiting it to January 2019. This signals regulatory concerns about governance and loan quality.

January 2019
Leadership Change

Ravneet Gill takes over as MD & CEO. Bank begins acknowledging asset quality issues with Gross NPAs rising to 7.39%.

March 2019
Capital Raising Struggles

Bank reports Q4 FY19 loss due to massive provisioning. Multiple capital raise attempts fail as investor confidence erodes.

September 2019
Rating Downgrades Begin

Credit rating agencies downgrade Yes Bank's bonds and deposits, making fundraising even more difficult.

March 5, 2020
RBI Moratorium Imposed

FY 2021-2023
